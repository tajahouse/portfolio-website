Get to Know Me

Technology has always been a major part of my life. Growing up, my dad was always learning new things about computers from hardware to software. He built my first personal desktop computer which worked like a dream! The computer console had a clear face and LED lights that lit up all the colors of the rainbow. Seeing this piqued my interest in technology. I would ask a ton of questions and learn what I could from books I would find in my dad’s office. Although I had always liked technology, and my dad encouraged my interest, I was always discouraged by others about my interest. Girls aren’t known to be in technology. Girls shouldn’t be web developers or computer engineers. Hearing this so much slowly discouraged me from becoming a developer. I, instead, became a teacher. Although I was a math teacher, I would find myself wanting to learn more about technology. One day, I ran across a school/not school called Lambda. I researched about them, thought about it for a long time, procrastinated on starting because I thought I wouldn’t be able to do such a thing, until one day making the decision to give it a try. This was the best decision of my life because now, I am able to create work like what you see here.

My goal is to assist people with their websites for their businesses, blogs, educational platforms, and many more. As we all know, technology has become the forefront of our lives and how we function. As technology evolves, so does the developer. I am that chameleon coder.
